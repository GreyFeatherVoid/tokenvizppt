# Bauhaus Geometry

## Color system
- Background: warm off-white, pale cream, or soft paper yellow.
- Ink: black, navy, or deep blue.
- Accent: primary red, yellow, and blue with strict restraint.
- Surface: flat geometric blocks with hard edges and strong alignment.

## Visual character
Modernist poster energy: architectural, graphic, disciplined, and memorable.

## Typography
Use geometric sans-serif behavior, bold labels, and compact title blocks. Type should align with geometric structure.

## Layout language
Use circles, rectangles, rules, diagonals, modular grids, and poster-like tension. Composition can be bold but must remain readable.

## Data and shape language
Represent data as geometric sequences, stacked blocks, primary-color markers, or structured diagrams. Use shapes as the visual rhythm.

## Best for
Design, art history, product aesthetics, creative strategy, education, and bold conceptual talks.

## Avoid
Photo-heavy pages, soft gradients, glass panels, overly realistic illustration, and decorative clutter beyond geometry.
