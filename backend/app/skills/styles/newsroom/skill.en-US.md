# Newsroom Red

## Color system
- Background: white, light gray, or pale red-gray.
- Ink: black, dark slate, or deep red-black.
- Accent: urgent red for bars, labels, and key separators.
- Surface: news cards, timestamp strips, headline blocks, and information panels.

## Visual character
Editorial, urgent, structured, and information-dense without being cluttered.

## Typography
Use bold headline hierarchy, compact labels, and clear body text. Headlines should read like news judgments.

## Layout language
Use vertical red bars, ticker-like labels, timestamp motifs, stacked news cards, map-like blocks, and crisp sectioning.

## Data and shape language
Use ranking blocks, fact boxes, quote strips, comparison panels, and clear source-like labels.

## Best for
Reports, announcements, event analysis, policy briefings, risk updates, and media-style summaries.

## Avoid
Soft pastel moods, playful geometry, dark cyber styling, weak headlines, and decorative image filler.
