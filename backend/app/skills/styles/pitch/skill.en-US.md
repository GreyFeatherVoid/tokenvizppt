# Venture Pitch

## Color system
- Background: crisp white, dark ink, or high-contrast gradient field.
- Ink: near-black or white depending on canvas.
- Accent: confident green, orange, blue, or red used for traction and momentum.
- Surface: bold cards, investor memo panels, and traction blocks.

## Visual character
Fast, decisive, investor-ready. The deck should communicate momentum and clarity without looking chaotic.

## Typography
Use bold sans-serif headlines, strong numbers, compact body text, and direct labels. Titles should sound like claims, not categories.

## Layout language
Use problem-solution frames, market wedge diagrams, traction cards, growth ladders, competitive matrices, and crisp strategic blocks.

## Data and shape language
Use large metrics, progress bars, TAM/SAM/SOM-style structures, before/after blocks, and callout badges. Make evidence easy to scan.

## Best for
Startup fundraising, product strategy, go-to-market, business model, and growth narratives.

## Avoid
Academic density, soft corporate neutrality, vague metaphors, tiny footnotes, and overcomplicated diagrams.
