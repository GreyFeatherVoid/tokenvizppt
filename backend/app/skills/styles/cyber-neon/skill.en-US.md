# Cyber Neon

## Color system
- Background: deep black, navy, or layered night gradient.
- Ink: near-white with cool gray secondary text.
- Accent: neon cyan, lime, magenta, or electric blue.
- Surface: HUD panels, luminous rules, dark glass cards, and grid overlays.

## Visual character
Cinematic, futuristic, interface-like, but still presentation-readable.

## Typography
Use technical sans-serif or mono-inspired labels. Headlines can be sharp and glowing only slightly. Avoid long paragraphs.

## Layout language
Use HUD frames, scanning lines, node maps, terminal-like panels, security dashboards, and cinematic depth. Keep one main glow source.

## Data and shape language
Use status chips, network nodes, thin neon connectors, small multiples, and signal bars. Keep contrast high.

## Best for
AI, cybersecurity, gaming, future systems, robotics, infrastructure, and speculative technology.

## Avoid
Over-glow, unreadable neon text, random sci-fi decoration, cute icons, and bright white corporate slides.
