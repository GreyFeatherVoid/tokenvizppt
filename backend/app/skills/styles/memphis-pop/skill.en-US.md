# Memphis Pop

## Color system
- Background: bright cream, pastel gradient, or playful color field.
- Ink: dark navy or black for control.
- Accent: yellow, purple, pink, cyan, orange, or lime, limited to deliberate motifs.
- Surface: bold cards, geometric stickers, dots, squiggles, and playful panels.

## Visual character
Energetic, youthful, brand-forward, and fun, but still controlled and readable.

## Typography
Use bold sans-serif titles, playful labels, and short body text. Type can be chunky but must not become childish.

## Layout language
Use offset cards, playful geometry, sticker-like accents, layered panels, and dynamic asymmetry. Balance energy with whitespace.

## Data and shape language
Use colorful ranking chips, playful but readable comparison cards, bold numbers, and simple icon-free visual markers.

## Best for
Youth brands, social campaigns, creative workshops, consumer products, education, and playful storytelling.

## Avoid
Overcrowding, childish clipart, corporate seriousness, too many colors at once, and low-contrast text.
