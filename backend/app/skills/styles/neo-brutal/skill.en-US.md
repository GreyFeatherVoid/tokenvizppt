# Neo Brutal

## Color system
- Background: warm cream, pale yellow, or flat off-white.
- Ink: black or near-black.
- Accent: loud orange, red, yellow, cobalt, or lime in controlled blocks.
- Surface: thick bordered cards, hard offset shadows, and flat color panels.

## Visual character
Raw, tactile, direct, and high-confidence. It should feel intentionally rough, not careless.

## Typography
Use bold sans-serif titles, heavy labels, and direct short statements. Type can be loud but must remain readable.

## Layout language
Use stacked cards, hard shadows, oversized labels, sticker-like but controlled blocks, and strong borders. Make hierarchy obvious.

## Data and shape language
Use chunky metric cards, high-contrast badges, progress blocks, and simple diagrams with heavy outlines.

## Best for
Startup energy, bold product stories, social campaigns, youth brands, opinionated strategy, and memorable announcements.

## Avoid
Subtle glassmorphism, soft corporate restraint, delicate gradients, thin elegant lines, and too many competing loud blocks.
